Car Price Prediction Classification

May 2023
Solely

The process began by obtaining a dataset from Kaggle and utilizing Python, along with the Pandas library, to clean the data by removing unnecessary columns. Numerical values were mapped to 1 if above the median and 0 otherwise, while research on Google helped identify factors influencing car pricing for handling categorical variables. The main goal was to predict car prices, which were mapped based on their median. This enables informing customers if their desired car's price is above or below the median, accounting for variations in car prices over time and across locations. The dataset was then split using the train_test_split function, with 20% allocated for testing. The model's accuracy was evaluated, resulting in a 87.88 score. To gain insights and aid in decision-making, the data and model results were visualized using Classification view with Matplotlib and Seaborn. The model was deployed on GitHub, making it ready for car price predictions and assisting potential car buyers.

Salary Prediction

June 2023
Solely

In this project, I, as a Data Scientist, built a Salary Prediction Model using Linear Regression. I used a dataset from Kaggle and prepared it for analysis by organizing the data. The dataset was split into training and testing sets to ensure the model's accuracy on new data. I employed scikit-learn's LinearRegression to train the model and understand the relationships between factors and salaries. The model's performance was evaluated using the R-squared (R2) score, indicating a high accuracy of 97% on the training data and 89% on the testing data. To visually represent the model's accuracy, I created a scatter plot comparing actual salaries with predicted values, adding a line to illustrate the alignment between predictions and real salaries. In conclusion, my Salary Prediction Model using Linear Regression demonstrated its reliability and precision in estimating salaries. The thorough data preprocessing, evaluation, and visualizations made it a valuable tool for real-world salary estimations.
